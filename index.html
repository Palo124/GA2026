About GA

  • Official ESN decision-making event for sections and stakeholders.
  • Focus on strategy, knowledge exchange, and community priorities for 2026.
  • Mix of plenary sessions, workshops, and networking moments.
  • Hosted in Prague with a local organising committee.
  • Attendance by registered ESN delegates and invited guests.
