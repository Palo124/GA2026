Staff sign-in

The backend can require a checkpoint password before meal and dietary data is returned. After a successful sign-in, this browser stores a short-lived session token (sessionStorage) and sends it with each lookup.

The token is cleared when you tap Lock, when the session expires, or when the backend reports that sign-in is required again.

Lock session

Lock clears the stored session token and stops the camera. Scanning and manual lookup stay disabled until someone signs in again with the checkpoint password.

Use this when handing the device to another person or leaving the checkpoint unattended.

Participant results

After a successful scan or manual search, details open in a full-screen friendly modal. The summary below stays on the page so you can compare or scroll back.

Signed in
